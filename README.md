# IBM Workforce Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyses employee workforce data from IBM to understand factors influencing monthly income, job satisfaction, and employee retention. The analysis focuses on providing clear, business-oriented insights that can support workforce planning and employee engagement strategies.

## Objectives
- Analyse income differences across gender and marital status
- Examine job satisfaction across roles and overtime conditions
- Explore employee attrition and the impact of engagement and mentoring programs
- Translate analytical findings into practical business recommendations

## Tools & Skills Used
- Microsoft Excel (data analysis, validation, reporting)
- Descriptive and comparative analysis
- Workforce analytics
- Business interpretation and reporting

## Key Insights
- Income differences by gender and marital status were not statistically meaningful
- Job role had a significant impact on job satisfaction and attrition
- Engagement programs improved employee satisfaction
- Mentoring programs showed a strong positive impact on employee retention

## Files in This Repository
- `IBM Workforce Analysis.xlsx` – Dataset and analysis
- `IBM Workforce Analysis Report.docx` – Detailed business report and recommendations

## Outcomes
This project demonstrates the ability to analyse real-world workforce data, apply structured analytical thinking, and communicate findings clearly to support business decision-making.
